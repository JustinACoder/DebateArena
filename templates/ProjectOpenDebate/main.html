How it Works

1. Chose a Debate

Search or browse for a debate that interests you in the Debates section.
Whether you prefer to debate politics, social issues, or even whether pineapple belongs on pizza, we have a debate for you!

2. Request an Opponent

Our system will match you with an opponent that is interested in the same debate with the desired stance.

3. Debate Live

Once matched, you can start debating live with your opponent in a one-on-one chat.
Gotta go? No problem! Your discussions are saved so that you can continue the discussion later.

Features

Live One-on-One Chat

DebateArena offers a live one-on-one chat feature that allows you to debate with your opponent in real-time. This allows for a more engaging and dynamic debate experience.

Semantic Search for Debates

Our semantic search engine allows you to search for debates based on the meaning of the query rather than just the keywords. This allows for more accurate and relevant search results.

Statistics on Debates

DebateArena provides statistics on debates such as stance distribution, intensity of debates, and more. This allows you to gain insights into the debates and the community.

Live Debate Analysis

DebateArena offers live debate analysis features such as sophism detection, fallacy detection, and source credibility analysis. This allows you to evaluate the quality of the debate and the arguments presented.

AI Opponents

DebateArena offers AI opponents that you can debate with. These AI opponents are designed to provide a challenging and engaging debate experience. Test your skills against the AI and see how you stack up!

More to come...

DebateArena is constantly evolving and adding new features to enhance your debate experience. Stay tuned for more exciting updates!
